about the company

Mizaj is a bespoke tailor shop created in 2008 with a intention to create love, passion and artistry back to tailoring. We do this by inhibiting accurate, original, time-tested processes of making menswear. We don’t do this out of mere business interest as we strongly believe that these approaches still deliver the best and promising fit and beauty to men’s garments.

This means we hand-make our products with craftsmen who are passionate and committed to their skills, individuals who love what they do. We’re also very meticulous in picking every process tangled in our creative process and curating every material that we use in order to deliver a result with both a solid construction and a stunning fit.

Each product is given full care and attention since we highly respect every tailor-made item we produce. Our end product is top-notch, and we can bigheadedly say that we are the best in the business because we give care and detailed attention in all aspects of our operations.

our mission

Mizaj took pride in providing consumers with the finest clothing experience by inhabiting original methods. We are committed to high-quality products, employee care, limitations within the industry, and art, design and technology.
